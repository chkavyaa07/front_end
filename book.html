My Favorite Novel:Things Fall Apart!

Things Fall Apart is a 1958 novel by Nigerian author Chinua Achebe. It is Achebe's debut novel and was written when he was working at the Nigerian Broadcasting Corporation.The novel was first published in London by Heinemann on 17 June 1958. story,which is set in British Nigeria,centers on okonkwo,a traditional influential leader of the fictional Igbo clan Umuofia.

Things Fall Apart is regarded as a milestone in African Literature.It gained critical acclaim and popularity upon publication,and has been translated into over fifty languages. It was listed on Time's 100 Best English-language Novels from 1923 to 2005.
